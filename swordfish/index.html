Swordfish

Physics roguelike with sword and fish

Swordfish is a physics-based action game with roguelike elements in which you play as a fish who swings around an oversized sword. The controls are simple, but deliberately very unwieldy. I made it in my first year at Abertay.

It's simple, but honestly really good fun. I'm very happy with it.
