A tiny puzzle game for Android

The lanterns are all in the wrong place! Help the normal-sized witch fix them in this tiny puzzle game on your phone. It's like sudoku, kind of.

I made Tiny Witch by myself in a couple of months while also working on 2Kidz. It was made in GameMaker.

What I learned

I gained experience working with GameMaker and the mobile platform, and in research-based design practice. I was able to engage with game design as an art form: Tiny Witch is the product of my unique vision.

I wish I could have released the game on the Play Store, and maybe even on iOS, but I didn't have time (or money, in the case of iOS).
