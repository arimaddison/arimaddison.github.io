2 players 1 character beat-'em-up

After two kids fail to get tickets to the latest smash hit R-rated movie, they choose to stack up and fight their way to getting seats instead!

Run, kick, punch and block your way through hordes of disinterested cinema employees in this wacky new take on a couch co-op beat-'em-up. Work together to string dynamic combos - communication is key!

I was the producer on 2Kidz as part of a team. We made the game in Godot over three months. You can see the full credits on the itch.io page.
